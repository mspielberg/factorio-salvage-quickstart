Version: 0.1.0
Date: 2019-07-24
  Features:
    - Self-powered 2x2 furnace, 10x as fast as a steel/electric furnace. Not craftable.
    - Self-powered 2x2 mining drill that outputs only to a built-in chest. It is possible to pull from the chest with 2 inserters. Not craftable.
    - The player starts with 10 very fast construction robots that consume little energy. Not craftable.
    - Self-powered 2x2 roboport with built-in logistic storage chest. Slow charging rate and 2 charging slots, which should be plenty for the 10 starter construction bots. Not craftable.
